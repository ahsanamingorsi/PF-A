The problem

Students often search through endless WhatsApp groups, email threads, and scattered Google Drive links to find study materials. Materials get lost, versions are messy, and valuable resources stay hidden.

WhatsApp email threads drive links

The idea

We are exploring a central platform where teachers upload materials once — slides, notes, books, and past papers — and students can easily access them anytime, without digging through chats or outdated folders.

Why this survey?

The survey helps us understand how students currently find resources, what frustrations they face, and what you actually need. Your answers will guide a design that works for real students.
