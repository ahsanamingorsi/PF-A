About AskPaperGen

AskPaperGen is a smart examination paper generator built for educators and institutions. It allows you to create custom exam papers with full control over sections, question types, and formatting. Whether you need MCQs, short questions, or even images and tables inside questions, AskPaperGen delivers a clean, professional paper ready to print.

Key features

Section‑based paper

Organize questions into sections (Part‑A, Part‑B, etc.) with custom headings and marks.

MCQs & short questions

Support for multiple choice, short answers, and long questions – all in one paper.

Image & table support

Add diagrams, tables, or images inside questions to make exams more descriptive.

Printable / downloadable

Export as PDF or print directly — clean formatting with no extra clutter.

User‑friendly interface

Intuitive dashboard, drag‑and‑drop sections, real‑time preview.

Consistent formatting

Automatically applies uniform fonts, spacing, and numbering.
